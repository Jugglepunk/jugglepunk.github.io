Fixing_a_Flat (2018) - One Page Printable Zine, staples

 

This zine is designed to help the average rider through the process of an emergency bicycle tube repacement. My goal for his project was to make a zine that was: informational, small enough to carry while rdng a bike, and easily reproduceable. The zine was created with simple informational grpahics and short descriptions. The final zine is a single page front and back that can be dispributed as a .pdf, printed with a household printer, and includes asssembly instructions.
