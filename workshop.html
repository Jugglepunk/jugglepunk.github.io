Some thoughts on Aesthetic

I think that aesthetic comes as an afterthought to the idea. Aesthetic should be rugged, show a little of the inner workings, show the process. I like to show the stitches, the screws, the hard edges. One of my favorite things to do is look at something and try to figure out how it was made. I want to encourage people to do the same with the things that I make.
