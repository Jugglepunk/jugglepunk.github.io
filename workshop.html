(Some_Of)_My_Favorite_Tools

Clay_Pick

This is originally a tool for cutting and shaping clay, but is also useful as a general shop tool. Good for lifting masking or picking at anything that you cant reach with your fingers.

Stanley_Knife

My favorite knife. Indespesible for breaking down boxes or any other cuting needs. It folds up and looks like a normal pocket knife but uses boxcutter blade inserts so its never dull.

Butane_Soledering_Iron

My favorite soldering iron. Cordless and heats up fast.

Mini_Needlenose_Pliers

Great for reaching things in tiny places and small delicate work.

Earmuffs

Hearing is important and hearing loss is cumulative. I wear these things whenever I work with something loud including: air compressors, loud power tools, and my shop vac.
