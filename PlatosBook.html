Platos_Book

Plato’s Allegory depicts the transition from the cave where the only light is a small campfire to the surface where the sun shines down. A triangle is the simplest representation of fire. I took that fire and rotated it until it formed a circle, the simplest representation of the Sun. While the viewer “reads” through the book, they recreate the journey from the cave to the open air. This piece is meant to be interacted with.
