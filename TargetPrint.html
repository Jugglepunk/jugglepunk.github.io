6$_Target_Print (2018) - Lithography W/ Variation

 

Every piece of art, even commercial art, has a person behind it. This person is as human as everyone else. Every piece of art you buy has been labored over by a person, even the $6 print you bought from Target. For this print I took a design that I found for $6 on the Target website and copied it into a lithograph, then printed it to show the labor of the printing process.
