Hand_Mouse (2019) - 3D Prnt, Mouse

 

Influenced by the book Speculative Everything by Anthony Dunne and Fiona Ray. This object is an attempt to make a "well designed" mouse. By adding the impression of a handprint I try to intrface the digital with the organic. This bject started out as a clay inprint of my hand, then 3D scanned and printed.
